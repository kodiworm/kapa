About KAPA

On November 12, 2019, Kenya Agricultural Product Association will host a live-streamed video conferenceengaging audiences in Uganda, Kenya and Tanzania on climate resilient agribusinessdevelopment. The event will provide a learning and knowledge sharing platform for youngagri-entrepreneurs. Some of the value chains of focus will include but not limited to oil seeds,pulses, potatoes and grains.

The discussion will reflect and be centered on a clear understanding of smart agribusinessopportunities, mindset change, identification of climate smart financing options andstrengthening sustainable partnerships and collaboration among participating stakeholders.The e-Summit will therefore provide a learning and knowledge sharing platform for youngagri-entrepreneurs, allowing them to tap into the available climate smart investmentopportunities in order to build climate resilient agribusinesses.

Vision and Mission

KAPA's vision is a world free of hunger and malnutrition. Its mission is to provide research-based policy solutions that sustainably reduce poverty and end hunger and malnutrition.

What We Do

Global, regional, and national food systems face major challenges and require fundamental transformations. More than ever, responding to these challenges will require a systems-oriented, multidisciplinary approach to reshape food systems so they work for all people sustainably. KAPA's Strategy Refresh 2018 to 20202 builds on the strong base of work developed under the Institute's 2013 to2018 strategy and focuses on five strategic research areas:

  • Fostering Climate-Resilient and Sustainable Food Supply
  • Promoting Healthy Diets and Nutrition for All
  • Building Inclusive and Efficient Markets, Trade Systems, and Food Industry
  • Transforming Agricultural and Rural Economies
  • Strengthening Institutions and Governance
